About

The person
behind the work.

I'm Rajat Ghosal — raised in Bihar, building for the world. I started writing code because I wanted to make things that didn't exist yet. That drive never left.

Over 3+ years, I've worked across the full stack — from pixel-level design decisions to ML inference pipelines. My work lives at the intersection of rigorous engineering and intentional design: two disciplines most people treat as separate, that I believe are inseparable.

I'm particularly drawn to AI products — not for the hype, but for the genuine design challenge: how do you make machine intelligence feel human, trustworthy, and useful? That question keeps me up at night. In a good way.
